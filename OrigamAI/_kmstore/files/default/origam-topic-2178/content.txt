# Horizontal Axis

@system (2017-12-31 23:00:00Z):
   Horizontal Axis 
 Horizontal Axis element describes the properties of the X axis of a  Column  or  Line Chart . 
   Attributes 
 
 
 
 
  
  
 
 
 
 
 Name 
 Name of the element. 
 
 
 Caption 
 Name describing the values on the X axis. 
 
 
 Field 
 Enter the name of the field that contains the values displayed on the axis and that is used for the aggregation. 
 
 
 AggregationType 
 Type of the aggregation used on the values. Possible types are  Distinct ,  DateYear ,  DateQuarter ,  DateMonth ,  DateWeek  and  DateDay .
